Our History

Founded in 1988, Horizon Academy began with a vision to create a learning environment where students could thrive academically while developing strong character and leadership skills.

Over the decades, we have grown from a small campus with just 120 students to a comprehensive educational institution serving over 850 students from diverse backgrounds.

Throughout our evolution, we have maintained our commitment to educational excellence, innovation, and community service, establishing ourselves as a cornerstone of educational leadership in the region.
